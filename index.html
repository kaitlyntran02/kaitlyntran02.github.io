ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a 3rd year student in the Game Design and Development program at RIT. I have a passion for creating new and innovative games. I have not only learned various programming languages such as C# and C++, but I also have knowledge in web design, game design, and how to work in different types of teams using excellent communication. I have a strong desire to learn new things everyday as well as work with people who are also just as passionate about video games.

In my free time, I help run RIT's Game Symphony Orchestra club as one if its founding members. I first held the position of the club's first treasurer for 3 years, and I am now the president of the club as of the Fall 2020 semester. When I was treasurer, I did work ranging from signing off on invoice forms to thinking of new, innovative ways of fundraising for our increasingly growing club. As the current president, I manage board meetings and run Game Symphony Orchestra as a whole. Outside of the club, I enjoy hanging out with friends and working on new projects that involve anything related to video games.
